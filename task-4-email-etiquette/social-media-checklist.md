# Social Media Etiquette Guide for College Students

This checklist is designed to help students maintain a professional and safe online presence.

## The Do's
1. **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Always use 2FA to add an extra layer of security to your social media accounts.
2. **Review Privacy Settings:** Regularly check your settings to control who can see your posts and personal information.
3. **Think Before You Post:** Consider the long-term impact of your content, as digital footprints are permanent.
4. **Use Professional Visuals:** Use clear, professional profile pictures, especially on career-oriented platforms like LinkedIn.
5. **Credit Original Sources:** Always give proper credit when sharing someone else's work, ideas, or media.

## The Don'ts
1. **Share Sensitive Info:** Never post your home address, phone number, or current real-time location publicly.
2. **Engage in Cyberbullying:** Avoid public arguments, offensive language, or any behavior that could be seen as harassment.
3. **Post Without Permission:** Never tag or share photos/videos of others without their explicit consent.
4. **Spread Unverified Info:** Avoid sharing "fake news" or inflammatory links that have not been fact-checked.
5. **Ignore Security Alerts:** Never overlook notifications about unrecognized logins or password change requests.
